Woojoo Na

I am a PhD student at Tufts-DISC advised by Professor Bert Huang. My research focuses on machine learning, optimization and math. Specifically, I am interested in identifying mathematical structures in candidate solution space of weak supervision algorithms.

Previously, I received my B.A. in Mathematics at Oxford. Before Tufts, I have been advised by such wonderful advisors: Professor Thomas Lukasiewicz at Oxford-CS, Dr. Andrey Kormilitzin, at Oxford Mathematical Institute,, and Professor Christophe Petit at Oxford Mathematical Institute.
